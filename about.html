About Us

The Association of Indian Muslims of America (AIM), is a non-profit, non-political, IRS tax-exempt, social service, Non Governmental Organization (NGO), of Muslims from India who live in North America. AIM is a 501-c-3 tax-exempt organization in US. The basic objective of AIM is to work for the welfare of Indian Muslims and India. Founded in 1985 in Washington DC, AIM has grown to be a nationwide organization in North America.

AIM's major programs and accomplishments over the years are:
  • Education Program.
  • Relief and Rehabilitation Program
  • Public Relations and Information Program.
  • Program to improve harmony between Indian and Muslims and others.
  • Program to Observe Social, Religious and National Events.

Mission & Objectives

The Association of Indian Muslims of America (AIM) was founded and incorporated in Washington DC in August 1985, as a non-profit, non-political organization to serve the various causes of the muslims of India. It brings together and represents a large number of muslims with origin in various regions of India who live throughout North America. AIM is a 501-C-3 tax exempt organization. The mission of AIM is defined in its following objectives.

In the last 25 years AIM has dedicated itself to the pursuit of the above mission & objectives and has been very successful in bringing about positive results for the Indian muslims community.

  • Serve as a Non Governmental Organization assisting the muslims community in India and North America to help improve the level of education in the community.
  • Provide financial relief assistance to the muslims community in India in the event of natural disasters, sectarian violence and other disorders.
  • Promote friendly relations between Indian muslims and others.
  • Conduct advocacy and public relations for the accomplishments, problems and issues of the Indian muslims and to disseminate information about the same.
  • Organize events to observe the various Indian and American national events, Islamic events and humanitarian occasions.
